Hiking

Mt. Teneriffe

June 2015

In June 2015 I hiked Mt. Teneriffe which is 14 miles round trip and 4000ft elevation gain. This summit is in the Snoqualmie Mountain Range and has a 360 view at the peak. It has one of the best views east of any of the peaks in this area, making it a great sunrise hike, which is actually how I first did this hike. We started at 1:45am and summited around 4:30am.
